Tips on using SecureDrop

SecureDrop is a Tor Hidden Service which means that we can't identify you by your web traffic, but this protection only works while using our service. If you link to external documents or sites such as DropBox or Google Drive, it may be possible for us or others to identify you. If you want to upload documents larger than the maximum size, you can coordinate with one of the journalists to arrange for a second secure transfer method.
